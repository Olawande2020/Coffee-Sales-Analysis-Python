# COFFEE SALES ANALYSIS
### Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee sales data to uncover purchasing patterns, sales trends, and customer preferences across two coffee stores. The analysis was conducted using Python for data cleaning, exploration, and visualization.

### Objective
- Identify sales trends over time  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Determine popular coffee products  
- Provide business recommendations based on insights

### Tools
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Matplotlib & Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

### Dataset
The dataset was obtained from Kaggle and contains transaction-level coffee sales data with the following columns:
- date
- datetime
- cash_type
- card
- money
- coffee_name

## Key Analysis Questions
1. How do coffee sales change over time?
2. What time of day do customers buy coffee most?
3. Which coffee products are most popular?
4. Which products generate the highest revenue?
5. How do customers prefer to pay (cash vs card)?
6. What percentage of revenue comes from top products?

## Key Insights
- Coffee sales peak during specific hours, indicating commuter demand.
- A few coffee products account for the majority of total revenue (Pareto effect).
- Card payments dominate cash transactions, showing digital payment preference.
- Certain products are consistently top sellers across both stores.

## Recommendations
- Promote best-selling products through bundles to increase revenue.
- Introduce time-based promotions during low-sales hours.
- Encourage digital payments with loyalty rewards.
- Upsell premium coffee products to increase average transaction value.
